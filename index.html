WHAT IS TAEKWONDO?

Taekwondo is one of the most systematic and scientific Korean traditional martial arts, that teaches more than physical fighting skills. Taekwondo was developed during the 1940s and 1950s by various martial artists who combined elements of Karate with traditional Korean martial arts traditions such as Taekkyeon, Subak, and Gwonbeop.It is a discipline that shows ways of enhancing our spirit and life through training our body and mind.It is recognized as one of the oldest forms of martial arts in the world, reaching back over 2,000 years.The name was selected for its appropriate description of the art: Tae (foot), Kwon (hand), Do (art). Taekwondo is characterized by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping and spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques.

FAQ

-Are your classes bilingual?

Yes.Every class is taught in spanish and english. Both coaches speak fluently both languages. The secretary also speaks both language. Feel free to talk to us in whichever language so feel more comfortable with. We will help you out as much as we can.

 

-How often do athletes change color belts?

There is ten color belts before the black belt. It goes from white to white advance,yellow to yellow advance,green to green advance, blue to blue advance, red to red advance, and lastly, to black. Athletes change color belts according to their knowledge and advancement. Usually athletes change color belts every six months. Each athlete has to pass the color belt change test to see if he or she is prepared to move to a higher belt.

 

-How many days do each athlete practice?

Each athlete have three classes per week. The first two classes during the week,are to practice technique and how to spar, and the third class is on Saturday and is sparring day to put everything taught during the week in practice.

 

How long are practices?

Each practice is about an hour long. It may be a little longer some days depending on the work out. For black belts they are an hour and a half long and it might be longer depending on the work out.

 

Is there any promotions?

Yes! Right now we are giving a free new uniform with a white belt to every athletes who enrolls to our gym.
